#### Image Captioner

> Made with: Python, PyTorch, NumPy, nltk, CUDA
>
> ---
>
> Trained a recurrent neural network to caption images from the [Microsoft COCO dataset](https://cocodataset.org/#home). The network consists of a pre-trained ResNet50 network and a series of LSTM cells, where teacher forcing is used to generate a softmax distribution of possible captions. After training, the network yeilds an average BLEU score of 64 over 3,000 images and 15,000 captions.
